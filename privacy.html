PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ninjalens ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with your use of our service available at ninjalens.net (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address provided at the time of purchase via LemonSqueezy.
  • License information: License key and instance ID used to authenticate your subscription.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics data collected via Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors interact with our website (page views, session duration, referral source).
  • Communication data: Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting us for support.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Ninjalens is designed with privacy as a core principle. The following data is explicitly never collected or stored by us:

  • Your GA4 analytics data or your clients' GA4 data
  • Your Gemini API key or Google credentials
  • Your Slack Webhook URLs
  • Any personal data belonging to your clients or end users
  • Contents of AI-generated reports

All GA4 data processing occurs within your own Google Account. Your Gemini API key is stored only in your Google Spreadsheet and is never transmitted to Ninjalens servers.
